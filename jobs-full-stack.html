Full Stack Developer

Basic requirements:

  • Strong knowledge of JavaScript (ECMAScript 5/6/7) and TypeScript.
  • Good understanding of client-side scripts and JavaScript frameworks, including Angular, React, Vue.js, etc.
  • Excellent understanding of web pages layout, including HTML5, CSS3.
  • Understanding of server platforms pre-processing CSS 3, such as LESS and SASS.
  • Experience with Twitter Bootstrap, Foundation, Material UI.
  • Knowledge Of Photoshop.
  • Experience in developing web sites and PHP programming 1+ year.
  • Knowledge of Php (Laravel, Yii1/2, Symfony, CodeIgniter, Zend), nodejs (Express, Koa).
  • Strong understanding and practical use of SQL (MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQLite).
  • Knowledge Of Nosql (MongoDB, Redis, CouchDB, DynamoDB).

Will be a plus:

  • English language: (Upper) Intermediate level.
  • Experience of using version control systems: Git, mercurial, svn.
  • Skills of work with Docker, docker-compose.
  • Work with payment systems: PayPal, Stripe API.
  • Understanding and experience with cloud platforms: AWS, Google Cloud Platform, Azure.
  • Experience with CMS systems: WordPress, Business catalyst, Joomla, OpenCart and Magento (would be a plus), including plugins, modules and components for them.

  • Opportunities for ongoing professional, career, and personal growth.
  • Stable salary on time.
  • Company care (a well-deserved rest, coffee and cakes, team building).
  • Friendly atmosphere in the team, support and assistance from colleagues and management.
  • Comfortable working place in modern office.
