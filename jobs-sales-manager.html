IT Sales Manager

Basic Requirements:

  • Written and oral English not less than Upper-Intermediate level.
  • Good oral and written Russian language skills.
  • Knowledge of the IT market, technologies and alternatives.
  • Basic understanding of web development and web marketing.
  • Experience in working with online freelance platforms, as well as outside of them.
  • Knowledge of the General theory of sales.
  • Effective communication skills, ability to establish business relations.
  • Good communication skills.
  • Stress resistance.
  • Proactivity and self-organization.
  • Focus on results.
  • Experience in a similar position in a related field.

Responsibilities:

  • Search and establish contacts with new customers.
  • Increase sales.
  • Working with documentation.
  • Sales planning and participation in the creation of the marketing strategy for the company.
  • Ebuilding long-term partnerships with clients.

Working conditions:

  • Five-day working week in a comfortable office in the city center.
  • Compensation and % on time.
  • Ability to attend training and internships.
  • Delicious lunch in the office: tea, coffee, cakes, etc.
  • A team of active, interesting and rising people.
  • Opportunities for ongoing professional, career, and personal growth.
